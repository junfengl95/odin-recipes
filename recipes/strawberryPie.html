Strawberry Pie

This is a recipes for strawberry pie


Ingredients
  • 1 quart fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons conrnstarch
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
Steps
  1. Gather all
  2. Arrange 1/2 strawberries in baked pastry shell. Place remaining strawberries in a medium saucepan
  3. Add sugar to the strawberries in the saucepan; place over medium heat and bring to a boil, sirring frequently.
  4. Whisk conrnstarch and water together in a small bowl. Gradually stir cornstarch mixture cornstarch mixture into boiling strawberry mixture. Reduce heat and simmer mixture until thickened, about 10 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. Pour cooked strawberry mixture over berries in pastry shell. Refrigerate until chilled and set, at least 3 hours.
  6. Before serving, whip cream in a medium bowl until soft peaks form.
  7. Serve each slice of pie with a dollop of whipped cream. Enjoy!
